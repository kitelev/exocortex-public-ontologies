object: "\"This annotation property links a subproperty of prov:wasInfluencedBy with the subclass of prov:Influence and the qualifying property that are used to qualify it. 

Example annotation:

    prov:wasGeneratedBy prov:qualifiedForm prov:qualifiedGeneration, prov:Generation .

Then this unqualified assertion:

    :entity1 prov:wasGeneratedBy :activity1 .

can be qualified by adding:

   :entity1 prov:qualifiedGeneration :entity1Gen .
   :entity1Gen 
       a prov:Generation, prov:Influence;
       prov:activity :activity1;
       :customValue 1337 .

Note how the value of the unqualified influence (prov:wasGeneratedBy :activity1) is mirrored as the value of the prov:activity (or prov:entity, or prov:agent) property on the influence class.\"@en"
